LDA 22 – RJTT

There may be no better exaple of an Lda approach, with the nav-aid being over 5.5 kilometers from the runway threshold. The most remarkable part of this approach to me (Other than the offset) is the missed apprach procedure. I cant think of any IAP at a code F airport that has you going missed at allmost 180 degrees to the approach path.

Overview

The LDA 22 approach at RJTT is a Localizer-type Directional Aid approach. It is classified as difficult due to terrain and high traffic. This page includes charts, approach notes, and visual references.
