Instrument Approach Collection

A curated collection of fascinating, unusual, and challenging instrument approach procedures from around the world — visualized, annotated, and ready to explore.

View Approach Gallery

Purpose

Aviation is full of standard procedures — but every now and then, a published approach stands out. Whether due to terrain, airspace, creativity, or history, some instrument procedures simply have character.

This project exists to document and share them in one place, complete with visual references and background notes.

What You'll Find

Complex Terrain

Procedures that carve between mountains, cliffs, and ridges in a way that challenges both pilots and designers.

Historic Approaches

Famous or infamous arrivals that have earned a place in aviation lore.

Creative Design

Approaches that make unorthodox use of navaids, clever altitude planning, or unusual route structure.

Global Collection

Procedures from all over the world, from massive hubs to airstrips tucked in the wilderness.

Created for educational and enthusiast purposes — not for flight use.
